How We Help Students

The HEU Guidance and Counseling Office delivers Services through Telebehavioral Health Technology, where the usual standards of care applied by Guidance Counselors when working wiht students still apply utilizing digital platforms that provide secure messaging to connect with a student.

Counseling

Counseling helps students navigate college life, make informed decisions, and foster personal growth. Students seeking individual support are encouraged to book an online appointment

Empathy Circle

This is a form of group counseling and psychosocial support that can be available for a group of five to eight individuals at a given virtual time.

Prevention and Wellness Activities

These programs address student needs identified through the latest Needs Assessment Survey (NAS) and focus on self-development through webinars, digital activities, learning materials, and videos.

Psychological Testing

Annual psychological tests help counselors assess students' interests, values, aptitudes, and career needs. Enrolled students can access the testing platform through a provided link.
